Professional Work and Impact

As part of my professional journey, I have conceived, led, and contributed to numerous high-impact products, projects, and product features across various domains. One of my notable contributions led to a patent filing by my company. My work has gained significant visibility, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic, where several of my initiatives received widespread attention for their impact on public health and healthcare delivery. Some of these key contributions are highlighted below.

Covid Vaccination App

The Covid Vaccination App is software solution that is the de facto product used to administer vaccines across the nation. Paired with its staffing and billing services, Carbon would be the preferred comprehensive solution to administer vaccines on behalf of cities across the U.S.

Covid Ready

Carbon Health – Covid Ready is a full end-to-end program designed to help businesses get their employees back to the workplace safely while providing education, monitoring, testing, management, and containment protocols for employers for use with their employees.

AI Charge Navigator

The AI Charge Navigator is a feature designed to streamline the process of populating charge-related fields in medical documentation. By leveraging Artificial Intelligence, this tool significantly reduces manual data entry and improves the accuracy of medical billing and coding.

AI Charting

Hands free charting built into Carbon's AI enabled EHR (Carby) that saves billions of key strokes and thousands of man hours saved
